Overcoming Discord Kitten Separation Anxiety (FOR VALORANT PALYERS)

My video game of choice is "Valorant", I strive to assist the Palyers of this game in their mission to touch grass and to transform them into productive members of society! (challenge impossible)

I have formulated the best steps to get them out of their shell in this step-by-step guide:

  • Set up a second monitor and place your Discord kitten's webcam on it. Now they're always with you!
  • Record your kitten's meows and use them as voice chat sound effects whenever you feel lonely
  • Print out a life-sized cardboard cutout of your Discord kitten for IRL interactions
  • Take breaks to cuddle your cardboard cutout kitten. It's surprisingly comforting.

Now here comes the hard part -- Touching grass!

  • Exit your room. This may be challenging, but trust us, it's worth it.
  • Take a shower AND brush your teeth so you get rid of the Viper Ultimate around you
  • Put on sunscreen. Your screen isn't the only thing that should be protected from rays.
  • Try to bunny-hop in real life. Spoiler: It's less effective.
  • Admire the beautiful graphics, also known as "real life."
  • Find some grass and gently touch it. Congratulations, you've done it!

If you still have seperation anxiety after doing all the steps above, then I have to say that there is a last resort! "Contact Us" by clicking on the button at the top!
